Compared to other mediums like online courses and podcasts, I find books to be the best media format to obtain an education quickly.

Below is a list of all books I've read in 2022.


1. The Theory of Everything (Stephen Hawking) - 01/06/2022
2. Biology Essentials for Dummies (Rene Fester Kratz and Donna Rae Siegfried) - 01/10/2022
3. BioBuilder: Synthetic Biology in the Lab (Natalie Kuldell, Rachel Bernstein, Karen Ingram, and Kathryn M Hart) - 02/04/2022
4. Fast Track Biology: Essential Review for AP, Honors, and Other Advanced Study (The Princeton Review) - 02/07/2022
5. A Cool Brisk Walk Through Discrete Mathematics (Stephen Davies) - 02/27/2022
6. Astrobiology: A Very Short Introduction (David Catling) - 04/14/2022
