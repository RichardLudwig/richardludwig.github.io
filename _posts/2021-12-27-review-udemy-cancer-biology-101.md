This short <a href="https://www.udemy.com/course/cancer-biology-101/" target="_blank">Udemy</a> course may be open to beginners, but some work is required before enrolling if you want to fully appreciate this course. 

Although the course is only 1 hour long, you will need to be comfortable with the basics of biology. 

No specific biology knowledge is required beforhand, but you will need to understand basic terminology and processes.

Regardless of perceived difficulty, <a href="https://www.udemy.com/user/emma-nichols-2/" target="_blank">Dr. Emma Nichols, PhD</a>, is very knowledgeable and explains cancer biology basics in interesting and simple ways to understand.

If you have never taken a biology course before, I recommend reading <a href="https://www.wiley.com/en-us/Biology+Essentials+For+Dummies-p-9781119589549" target="_blank">Biology Essentials for Dummies</a> prior to this Udemy course to gain a sense of the subject.
